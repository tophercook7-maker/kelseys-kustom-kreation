# Brand Guidelines
## Kelsey's Kustom Kreation

### Brand Philosophy
Premium, confident, boutique-quality custom products. Not mass-made. Kustom-made.

### Primary Tagline
**"Where Your Ideas Come Alive"**

### Secondary Tagline
**"Not Mass-Made. Kustom-Made."**

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Light, Regular weights)
- **Usage**: Clean, minimal, elegant spacing
- **Headings**: Light weight (300), generous letter-spacing
- **Body**: Regular weight (400), comfortable line-height

### Color Palette
- **Background**: Full-canvas image with subtle overlays
- **Text**: White with soft shadows for readability over images
- **Glass Elements**: Frosted glass with 70% white opacity, blur effects
- **Accents**: Minimal, restrained use of color

### Visual Style
- No boxed sections
- No solid-color blocks
- Continuous background image throughout
- Subtle frosted-glass overlays only where needed
- Medium-light typography
- Elegant spacing
- Restrained motion (slow fades, parallax scroll)
- Zero gimmicks

### Logo Usage
- Use SVG version for scalability
- Maintain minimum clear space around logo
- Logo should be readable on both light and dark backgrounds
- Use PNG versions when SVG is not supported

### Photography Style
- Premium, realistic lighting
- Neutral color tones
- High-quality product mockups
- Lifestyle images used sparingly
- Consistent visual language across all imagery

### Voice & Tone
- Confident
- Premium
- Personal
- Professional
- Approachable
